Recommendation
Route & itinerary
Both directions presented in full. Toggle to compare. Recommended route marked with ★.
Flight reference: Outbound Fri May 22 — HKG 20:55 → arrive next morning (PRG 07:55 or WAW 08:10). Return Sun May 31 — depart evening (WAW 21:15 or PRG 19:30) → HKG next day.
Why PRG → KRK → WAW is recommended
  • Kraków lands on Wed–Fri. The three cleanest days — every site at full hours, zero closures.
  • Prague's Saturday Jewish Quarter closure absorbed on arrival day when you'd be easing in.
  • Warsaw's Tuesday closures avoided — your Warsaw days are Sat–Sun.
  • Long travel leg mid-trip (Prague→Kraków ~6h) when energy is higher. Short Pendolino (~2.5h) for final transition.
  • Late WAW departure (21:15) maximises the final day.
  • Sunday Cathedral timing solved naturally. Do other Castle buildings 9am, enter St. Vitus at noon.
WAW→PRG is also viable. Its main trade-off: Kraków falls on Mon–Wed with Monday Wawel limitations. Toggle to compare.
